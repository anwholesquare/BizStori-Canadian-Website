Download the App

Begin your Circlebet journey by downloading our user-friendly app from your device's app store. Whether you're using a smartphone or tablet, the app is designed to provide a seamless and enjoyable betting experience at your fingertips.

Frequently Asked Questions

Come and have a look at why we are different and how we are trying to change the industry for the better.

About my personal and financial information?

Circlebet employs cutting-edge encryption to secure your personal and financial data, ensuring privacy and confidentiality throughout your betting activities.

Multiple private betting circles

Yes, you can create and customize multiple private betting circles, tailoring each one to specific preferences and interests within your various circles of friends, family, or colleagues.

Ethical betting tools

Circlebet offers tools such as betting limits, break options, and access to support resources to help users and their circle members manage their betting activities responsibly.

Any fees associated with transactions?

It aims to keep transactions hassle-free. While there may be standard payment processing fees, we strive to provide transparent information about any associated costs during transactions.

Fair and transparent betting practices

It maintains complete transparency by providing clear information on odds and potential outcomes. The platform is designed to uphold fair betting practices, ensuring an honest and trustworthy environment.

Available on both iOS and Android devices?

Yes, Circlebet is available for download on both iOS and Android devices. You can find the app on the respective app stores for a seamless betting experience.

Encounter issues or have questions while using Circlebet?

Our dedicated customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you. You can reach out for help through the in-app support feature or contact us via email or phone, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable betting experience for you and your circle members.
