Client Background

Dental Excellence Group is a well-established dental practice in Ontario. They approached Bizstori with a goal to enhance their online presence and attract new patients in a competitive market.

Challenges

Strong competition from nearby dental practices
Outdated website with low visibility
Need to increase patient appointments

Our Solution

Frequently Asked Questions

Use customer data to build great and solid product experiences that convert.

What is pharmacy marketing?

Pharmacy marketing refers to the strategic promotion and advertising of pharmacy services, products, and healthcare offerings to attract and engage patients, increase brand awareness, and drive business growth.

Why is digital marketing important for pharmacies?

Digital marketing is essential for pharmacies in today’s digital age as it allows them to reach a wider audience, increase online visibility, engage with patients through various digital channels, promote services and products, and build strong relationships with their target market.

What services does your pharmacy marketing agency provide?

Our pharmacy marketing agency offers a range of digital marketing services tailored specifically for pharmacies. This includes website design and development, search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing, content creation, email marketing, online advertising, and analytics and reporting.

How can digital marketing help my pharmacy attract new patients?

Through effective digital marketing strategies, we can help your pharmacy increase its online visibility, target potential patients through precise audience targeting, create engaging and informative content, and leverage various digital channels to drive traffic and conversions, ultimately attracting new patients to your pharmacy.

Can you help my pharmacy with online reputation management?

Yes, we offer online reputation management services to help pharmacies build and maintain a positive online reputation. We monitor and respond to online reviews, manage online listings and directories, and implement strategies to enhance your pharmacy’s online credibility and reputation.

How do you measure the success of pharmacy marketing campaigns?

We utilize various analytics and tracking tools to measure the success of our pharmacy marketing campaigns. We track key performance indicators such as website traffic, conversions, engagement metrics, online visibility, and ROI to provide you with comprehensive reports and insights into the effectiveness of our strategies.

How can I get started with your pharmacy marketing services?

Getting started with our pharmacy marketing services is easy. Simply reach out to us through our contact form or contact information provided on our website, and our team will schedule a consultation to discuss your pharmacy’s specific marketing needs and goals. We’ll then create a customized strategy tailored to your requirements and objectives.
